AFTER EFFECTS
What if your car could provide power during an outage? This animation, created as part of an e-course for the SCALE project, highlights the global energy crisis and the challenges of unreliable power. It explores how V2X technology can bridge this gap by turning vehicles into mobile power sources, enhancing energy resilience and security in regions with frequent outages.
