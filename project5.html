APP DESIGN
CiviComm stands for Civic – “relating to a citizen” – and Community. The aim is to focus on empowering communities to take ownership of their city. Through this app, citizens can report problems in their neighborhood and simultaneously get involved as volunteers. The app offers volunteer opportunities published by local groups, individual citizens, and government authorities. Its primary goal is to promote a sense of shared responsibility between citizens and the state.
