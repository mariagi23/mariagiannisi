EDITORIAL
WE DRIVE CHANGE is a project aimed at empowering women through education, employment, and advocacy within the rapidly evolving field of e-mobility.

The initiative focuses on promoting sustainable, inclusive, and gender-equitable mobility solutions, driving the transition towards cleaner transportation systems that are accessible to all. By fostering partnerships with governments, universities, NGOs, and the private sector, the project works to create opportunities for women to actively participate in the development and implementation of innovative e-mobility solutions. Through this work, WE DRIVE CHANGE aims to contribute to global climate action while ensuring women are equally represented in shaping the future of sustainable transportation.
