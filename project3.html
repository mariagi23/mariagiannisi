The Urban Living Lab Center is jointly operated by the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), the Technical University of Berlin (TUB), and the Wuppertal Institute, and builds on collaboration with UN-Habitat. The goal is to build upon a range of joint projects and create a platform for other stakeholders and projects in this field to expand and support the program. The designs were published on the organization's social media accounts.
